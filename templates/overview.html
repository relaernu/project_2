Victoria Crash Statistics

Overview

CrashStats data is made available by VicRoads and provides information about road crashes in Victoria for educational purposes. The dataset contained accident details from January 2006 to November 2020. This data allows users to analyse the correlation between driver, vehicle, weather and area that was involved in a crash. It can also be used by an insurance company to determine the prices of premiums to quote a particular individual. The more accident prone the individual is, thus the higher the premium. Or which roads and what suburbs have the most crashes. Furthermore, local councils can use the data to identify the roads that need fixing.

Dashboard Design

During our brainstorming session, we googled and reviewed existing dashboards, and subsequently dashboards specifically relating to road accidents. As we have extensive data, we decided to create an interactive dashboard as it allows users to filter and explore the data from different perspectives or in more detail. For visualisations, we have created maps, bar charts and line graphs.
